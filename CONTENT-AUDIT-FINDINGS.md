## Priority 4: Content Quality Improvements

### Brand Voice Issues to Address:

#### Currently Missing:
1. **Specific quantitative details**: Add timeframes, typical costs, market ranges
2. **Real-world examples**: Add scenario-based illustrations
3. **Practical implementation guidance**: Add "how-to" sections
4. **Trade-off discussions**: Add balanced perspective on alternatives
5. **Current market context**: Update with 2024-2025 practices

#### Writing Quality Issues (found in shorter articles):
1. Overly generic statements without specifics
2. Lack of actionable takeaways
3. Missing subsections for major topics
4. Weak conclusions that don't synthesize key points

### Recommended Additions for ALL Articles:

1. **Add "Common Challenges" Section**:
   - What problems do practitioners face?
   - What mistakes should be avoided?

2. **Add "Implementation Checklist" or "Action Items"**:
   - Practical steps readers can take
   - Sequenced approach to implementation

3. **Expand "Key Takeaways"**:
   - Currently too brief (4-5 bullets)
   - Should be 8-10 comprehensive bullets summarizing article

4. **Add Quantitative Benchmarks**:
   - Typical costs, timeframes, ranges
   - Market standards and norms
